Craftsmanship

Tekton is an Ancient Greek term for an artisan-- a carpenter, wood-worker or builder. In modern scholarship, the word sometimes is re-interpreted or translated as craftsman.

21st Century digital craftspersons stand before great opportunities to develop elegant solutions to the complex problems of the world. We are not training enough people to fulfill the programming needs emerging within this hour, and as the next billion from the Global South prepares to enter the mobile revolution, demands for additional onramps to contextual engagement will require a new breed of empathic software developers.
